# Neldorild

## Overview
Neldorild is a major Mulhorandi settlement on internal trade and river routes, often treated as a practical counterweight to capital politics in [[settlements/skuld|Skuld]].

## Role in the Realm
It serves as a logistics and provisioning node for provincial governance and long-distance movement through Mulhorand.

## Power and Institutions
Regional governors, military quartermasters, and temple estates compete for influence over labor and transport.

## Campaign Use
Use Neldorild for convoy security, tax-dispute arbitration, and investigations into corruption inside supply networks.
