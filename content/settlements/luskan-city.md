# Luskan (City)

## Overview
A hard-edged northern port where sea power, private contracts, and rival command circles overlap.

## Role in the Realm
Primary settlement of [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]], acting as both harbor stronghold and political battleground.

## Power and Institutions
Ship captains, arcane authorities, dock syndicates, and hired enforcers all compete for leverage.

## Campaign Use
Use for maritime intrigue, port security conflicts, intelligence brokering, and northern route politics.
