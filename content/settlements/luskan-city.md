# Luskan (City)

## Overview
A hard-edged northern port where ship captains, hired steel, and arcane influence compete for control.

## Role in the Realm
Primary settlement of [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]], positioned as a maritime gate between northern waters and the wider [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]].

## Governance and Power
- Formal authority is often fragmented across captain blocs, harbor officers, and private contracts.
- Control of docks and fleets matters more than ceremonial title in most disputes.
- Outside powers regularly court local actors instead of negotiating only through official channels.

## District and Landmark Use
- Main docks and shipyards for crew recruitment, cargo seizures, and contract violence.
- Tavern and warehouse rows for intelligence trades, hostage leverage, and smuggling deals.
- Command compounds and arcane enclaves for succession intrigue and hidden policy decisions.

## Economy and Daily Life
- Privateering, shipping security, salvage, and cold-water trade define local wealth.
- Labor is seasonal and volatile, with crews assembling quickly for high-risk work.
- Daily life is shaped by rumor networks, debt obligations, and who can guarantee protection.

## Immediate Adventure Value
- Strong fit for maritime intrigue, escort work, and "legal gray zone" enforcement jobs.
- Useful as a launch point for northern sea expeditions and black-market procurement.
- Supports faction play where alliances change fast after losses at sea.

## Hooks
- A captain coalition hires outsiders to prove a rival staged a pirate attack to void contracts.
- A seized cargo hold contains goods claimed by three cities under conflicting charters.
- A trusted harbor broker disappears before treaty talks, and every faction blames a different patron.
