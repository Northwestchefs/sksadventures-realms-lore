# Cimbar

## Overview
Cimbar is often treated as the leading city of [[nations-realms/chessenta|Chessenta]], though influence can shift with military and civic fortunes.

## Role in the Realm
It acts as a political bellwether for inter-city alliances, military appointments, and regional diplomacy.

## Power and Institutions
City magistrates, veteran commanders, and merchant patrons compete to direct policy and war footing.

## Campaign Use
Use Cimbar for coup rumors, mercenary contracting, and negotiations between rival city envoys.
