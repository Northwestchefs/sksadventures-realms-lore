# Selgaunt

## Overview
Major city and capital of [[nations-realms/sembia|Sembia]], known for mercantile politics, legal maneuvering, and contract-driven power.

## Role in the Realm
Selgaunt serves as a policy and finance center where trade law, investment houses, and civic authority overlap.

## Power and Institutions
Merchant magnates, chartered civic officials, and legal courts compete to set terms for who profits and who gets protected.

## Campaign Use
Strong for contract disputes, fraud investigations, debt politics, and diplomacy framed as commerce.
