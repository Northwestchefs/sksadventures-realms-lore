# Waterdeep (City)

## Overview
A major Sword Coast metropolis and one of the most influential trade and political centers in the Realms.

## Role in the Realm
Core urban engine of [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]], projecting influence through commerce, diplomacy, and information networks.

## Power and Institutions
Public order, guild structures, noble interests, and masked governance traditions all shape city life.

## Campaign Use
Ideal for intrigue, investigations, faction arbitration, and urban adventure arcs.
