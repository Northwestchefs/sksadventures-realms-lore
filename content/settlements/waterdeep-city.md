# Waterdeep (City)

## Overview
A premier Sword Coast metropolis where wealth, law, and hidden influence intersect at massive scale.

## Role in the Realm
Core settlement of [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]], projecting regional power through commerce, diplomacy, and information networks.

## Governance and Power
- Civic authority blends formal offices, legal institutions, guild leverage, and masked leadership traditions.
- Noble houses, merchant interests, and public order officials can align or clash by district.
- Enforcement is strong, but political outcomes often hinge on who controls process and narrative.

## District and Landmark Use
- Dock and market wards for customs disputes, cargo fraud, and guild arbitration.
- Noble and civic quarters for blackmail plots, legal maneuvering, and patron rivalries.
- Temple and entertainment districts for rumor collection, social influence, and discreet meetings.

## Economy and Culture
- Maritime trade, banking, high craft, and contract law drive city priorities.
- A cosmopolitan population supports diverse institutions, shrines, and social circles.
- Status, etiquette, and reputation can carry as much weight as coin in key negotiations.

## Immediate Adventure Value
- Excellent for investigations, political intrigue, and faction-brokered dealmaking.
- Supports campaign arcs around law, permits, licenses, and controlled violence.
- Strong downtime hub for networking, acquisition, and strategic patron play.

## Hooks
- A lawful guild vote is manipulated through forged proxies, threatening citywide supply contracts.
- A missing magistrate's casebook could expose who profits from selective enforcement.
- A high-profile festival security plan masks a covert operation against a rival power bloc.
