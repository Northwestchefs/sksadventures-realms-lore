# Port Nyanzaru

## Overview
A major Chultan port and gateway for maritime trade, expeditions, and overland ventures into the peninsula interior.

## Role in the Realm
Primary political and economic hub of [[nations-realms/chult|Chult]] in many campaign eras.

## Power and Institutions
Merchant-charter interests, local civic leadership, and expedition logistics networks shape city priorities.

## Campaign Use
Best for expedition launch arcs, contract disputes, and diplomacy between local and foreign interests.
