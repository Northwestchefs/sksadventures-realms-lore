# Callidae
The hidden City of the Starlight Elves.

## Overview
Callidae is an ancient refuge shaped by Starlight Elf star-lore, where living crystal spires and elder roots were grown into a city over many ages. It serves as a sacred center of memory, ritual, and leadership for scattered Starlight Elf enclaves.

## Location
Callidae lies far beneath the world in a colossal cavern ecosystem lit by star-light crystals and bioluminescent flora. Rivers, root-bridges, and crystal groves connect the city to hidden tunnels and protected wild zones.

## Culture
Starlight Elf life in Callidae centers on:
- druidic star magic practiced through seasonal rites and sky-memory circles
- harmony with subterranean nature, with districts grown rather than built
- celestial alignment rituals timed to crystal constellations in the cavern vault
- preservation of ancient knowledge in living archives and oral star-chants

## Government
Callidae is governed by the [[factions/eldergrove-conclave|Eldergrove Conclave]], a three-branch civic body of rootspeakers, star sages, and civic keepers. Together they interpret omens, adjudicate petitions, and maintain long-cycle plans for the city and surrounding cavern realms.

### Governance in Practice
- district circles debate first, then escalate binding issues to Conclave assemblies
- emergency threats trigger Moonline Sessions for temporary decrees
- law and precedent are preserved in star-chants and crystal records
- security, ecology, and secrecy are treated as inseparable duties

## Districts
- [[callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]] — Terraced groves where luminous flora are cultivated for food, ritual, and healing.
- [[callidae-districts/crystal-canopy|Crystal Canopy]] — A high-walk district of living crystal branches that houses artisans and arcane crafters.
- [[callidae-districts/moonwell-terraces|Moonwell Terraces]] — Cascading pools and moonwells used for divination, cleansing rites, and diplomacy.
- [[callidae-districts/astral-observatory|Astral Observatory]] — A protected complex where star sages chart celestial currents and omen-paths.
- [[callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]] — A winding trade quarter built among vast roots, serving caravans from hidden realms.
- [[callidae-districts/eldergrove|Eldergrove]] — The oldest sacred quarter, centered on primordial trees and council circles.

## Defenses
Callidae is concealed behind layered illusion barriers, veiled tunnel routes, and wards keyed to Starlight Elf bloodlines. Living forest guardians, crystal sentinels, and bound druidic wards defend the city from intruders and corruption.

### Departure Protocol
- all sanctioned exits route through [[callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]]
- travelers formally declare destination and purpose before a gate-druid
- [[people/raelin-silverleaf|Raelin Silverleaf]], as Head Druid of the gardens, can delay or deny departure during quarantine, ward instability, or active threat windows

## Adventure Hooks
- A crystal constellation goes dark, and the council needs outsiders to recover a stolen star-seed from forbidden tunnels.
- Diplomats disappear during a moonwell rite, threatening a fragile alliance with a neighboring cavern realm.
- Rootmarket merchants report dream-plagues spread by tainted spores from a sealed grove.
- A young unicorn guardian has fled into ancient ruins after witnessing a prophecy it cannot explain.
