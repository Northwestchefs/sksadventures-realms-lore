# Suzail

## Overview
Capital city of [[nations-realms/cormyr|Cormyr]], known for royal administration, court politics, and military coordination.

## Role in the Realm
Suzail functions as the political heart of crown authority and a central node for taxes, decrees, and diplomacy.

## Power and Institutions
Royal offices, noble representatives, and military command circles intersect here, often in tension.

## Campaign Use
Strong for courtly missions, intrigue, legal disputes, and high-profile state assignments.
