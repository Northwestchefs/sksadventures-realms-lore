# Immilmar

## Overview
Capital of [[nations-realms/rashemen|Rashemen]], serving as a political, spiritual, and military coordination center.

## Governance and Power
- Local civic leadership operates alongside Rashemi cultural and spiritual authorities.
- Military commanders influence policy where border security is at stake.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Council Quarter: meeting halls and administrative compounds.
- Market Ring: caravans, craft goods, and winter storage exchanges.
- Guard Barracks: mustering grounds for regional defense forces.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Trade in furs, timber goods, and practical war supplies.
- Seasonal festivals and communal rites reinforce local identity.

## Adventure Use
- Escort emissaries to a tense council session on Thayan border threats.
- Track missing winter stores before a severe cold season hits.
- Uncover sabotage aimed at fracturing city leadership.

## Canon Notes
Sources vary on political details by era; keep who dominates city policy flexible for your campaign period.
