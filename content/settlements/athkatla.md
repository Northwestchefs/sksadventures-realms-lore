# Athkatla

## Overview
Athkatla is the capital of [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and the realm’s financial and political center. It is a city of counting houses, dockside intrigue, elite patronage, and constant legal maneuvering.

## Campaign Use
Use Athkatla for merchant politics, investigations into corruption, contract disputes, espionage, and faction play tied to the [[factions/council-of-six|Council of Six]].
