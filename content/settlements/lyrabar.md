# Lyrabar

## Overview
Capital port of [[nations-realms/impiltur|Impiltur]], functioning as court center, fleet anchor, and primary eastern gateway.

## Governance and Power
- Crown officials, noble representatives, and temple-backed institutions all influence policy.
- Harbor command and urban guard leadership coordinate security in periods of succession strain.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Royal Quarter: palace compounds, court offices, and noble residences.
- Harbor Chain: military docks, merchant piers, and bonded storage halls.
- Temple Rise: clustered major shrines tied to civic charity and law.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Strong revenue from shipping, naval provisioning, and high-value craft trade.
- Public culture blends formal court ritual with practical coastal mercantile habits.

## Adventure Use
- Escort witnesses in a noble succession hearing with citywide consequences.
- Hunt saboteurs targeting fleet stores before a defensive deployment.
- Broker peace between dockworkers and temple-charity overseers during shortages.

## Canon Notes
Impiltur's internal politics vary by era; adjust court factions and succession pressure to your campaign timeline.
