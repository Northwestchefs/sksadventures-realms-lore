# Hlondeth

## Overview
Hlondeth is a Chondathan power center with a reputation for layered local politics, guarded institutions, and intense control over who gets influence.

## Location
It sits along Chondath's inland-coastal exchange web, connecting river traffic, caravan routes, and regional administration.

## Civic Pattern
- tightly held ruling circles with strong patronage networks
- civic offices prioritizing security, surveillance, and selective access
- merchant actors working through intermediaries rather than open councils

## Pressure Points
- factional purges and counter-purges inside city offices
- covert competition over tax routes and intelligence access
- neighboring powers probing for leverage through trade dependencies

## Campaign Use
Use Hlondeth for cloak-and-ledger intrigue, informant extraction, and missions where visibility is a liability.
