# Telflamm

## Overview
A major port city of [[nations-realms/thesk|Thesk]] where sea trade, caravan flow, and covert commerce overlap.

## Governance and Power
- Merchant interests and customs officials dominate daily governance.
- Security forces focus on keeping trade moving while managing entrenched criminal influence.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Deepwater Docks: high-volume shipping and bonded warehouses.
- Caravan Gate: tariff checkpoints and overland freight yards.
- Lantern Ward: taverns, brokers, and rumor-heavy side markets.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Revenue depends on customs, warehousing, and route brokerage.
- Residents are used to layered loyalties: guild, caravan house, and city law.

## Adventure Use
- Audit cargo manifests tied to a smuggling ring with political cover.
- Guard a caravan consortium negotiation on new toll rights.
- Recover a vanished courier carrying treaty terms for eastern allies.

## Canon Notes
Telflamm is often depicted with strong underworld activity, but scale and control vary by source and era.
