# Memnon

## Overview
Major southern port of [[nations-realms/calimshan|Calimshan]] facing crowded sea lanes and constant commercial rivalry.

## Role in the Realm
Memnon is a maritime counterweight to [[settlements/calimport|Calimport]], channeling customs revenue, naval logistics, and coastal influence.

## Power and Institutions
Port governors, merchant syndicates, and military harbor commands compete to control contracts, tariffs, and shipping security.

## Campaign Use
Use Memnon for dockside intrigue, smuggling investigations, convoy escort jobs, and tense negotiations between sea captains and officials.
