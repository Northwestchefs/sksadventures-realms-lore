# Darromar

## Overview
Darromar is commonly presented as the royal center of [[nations-realms/tethyr|Tethyr]], where court politics, noble obligations, and civic administration meet.

## Location
It sits in Tethyr's western sphere, positioned to influence both inland feudal routes and coastal-facing policy.

## Civic Pattern
- crown institutions working through layered noble households
- court officials balancing reform agendas against entrenched privilege
- urban guild interests pressing for predictable law and trade stability

## Pressure Points
- noble succession disputes spilling into city governance
- provincial lords testing royal authority through tax and levy delays
- cross-border intrigue tied to [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and [[nations-realms/border-kingdoms|Border Kingdoms]] politics

## Campaign Use
Use Darromar for court petitions, diplomatic receptions, legal appeals, and missions where protocol is as dangerous as open conflict.
