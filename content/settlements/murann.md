# Murann

## Overview
Murann is a strategically important Amnian port where legal trade, maritime finance, and smuggling networks overlap. Competing investors and shipping interests make the city volatile and valuable.

## Campaign Use
Use Murann for sea-lane security missions, anti-smuggling operations, port politics, and covert work tied to rival houses and customs authorities.
