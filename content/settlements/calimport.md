# Calimport

## Overview
A major southern metropolis known for wealth concentration, elite competition, and dense trade traffic.

## Role in the Realm
Capital-scale anchor of [[nations-realms/calimshan|Calimshan]] and a key maritime gateway for the region.

## Power and Institutions
Court officials, merchant dynasties, military administrators, and temple networks influence civic order.

## Campaign Use
Strong for high-stakes trade disputes, political patronage stories, and multi-faction urban play.
