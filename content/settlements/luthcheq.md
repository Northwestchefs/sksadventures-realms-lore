# Luthcheq

## Overview
Luthcheq is a major Chessentan city known for hardline civic identity and strong military posture.

## Role in the Realm
The city is a frequent center of resistance to unification projects led by rival Chessentan powers.

## Power and Institutions
Militant civic councils, veteran officers, and local cult networks influence law, policing, and diplomacy.

## Campaign Use
Use Luthcheq for city-state brinkmanship, ideology-driven investigations, and missions between hostile urban factions.
