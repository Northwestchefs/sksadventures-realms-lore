# Unthalass

## Overview
Unthalass is the capital of [[nations-realms/unther|Unther]], where restoration-era authority is most publicly asserted.

## Role in the Realm
The city functions as the administrative and symbolic center for imperial claims and external diplomacy.

## Power and Institutions
Imperial loyalists, temple leadership, and military offices all contend to define legitimate governance.

## Campaign Use
Use Unthalass for treaty talks, legitimacy disputes, and high-stakes escort jobs tied to state ceremonies.
