# Arrabar

## Overview
Arrabar is a flagship trade city of [[nations-realms/chondath|Chondath]], known for port finance, warehouse politics, and river-to-sea commerce.

## Location
It anchors key approaches between inland trade arteries and the [[regions/sea-of-fallen-stars|Sea of Fallen Stars]].

## Civic Pattern
- merchant councils shaping policy through contracts and credit
- harbor bureaucracies controlling dock access, inspections, and fees
- neighborhood militias and watch captains focused on market order

## Pressure Points
- insurance fraud, cargo disputes, and forged manifests
- rivalry with neighboring ports in [[nations-realms/sespech|Sespech]] and [[nations-realms/turmish|Turmish]]
- political deadlock when guild blocs split over war-risk shipping

## Campaign Use
Use Arrabar for shipping disputes, commercial espionage, debt enforcement jobs, and tense negotiations over contested cargoes.
