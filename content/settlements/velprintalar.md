# Velprintalar

## Overview
Coastal capital of [[nations-realms/aglarond|Aglarond]], positioned as a defensive and diplomatic hinge on the eastern Sea of Fallen Stars.

## Role in the Realm
Velprintalar coordinates crown policy, harbor security, and responses to pressure from neighboring powers.

## Power and Institutions
City authorities, naval officers, and local lords shape policy through a mix of frontier pragmatism and court caution.

## Campaign Use
Useful for border intelligence missions, convoy security, and tense diplomatic summits.
