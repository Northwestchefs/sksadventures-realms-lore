# Baldur's Gate (City)

## Overview
A major port city marked by intense class contrast, competitive commerce, and persistent faction pressure.

## Role in the Realm
Primary settlement of [[nations-realms/baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]], where governance, trade, and criminal economies overlap.

## Power and Institutions
Merchant elites, civic officers, district enforcers, and temple charities all compete to shape local order.

## Campaign Use
Useful for street-level intrigue, corruption investigations, and faction-brokered negotiations.
