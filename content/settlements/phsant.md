# Phsant

## Overview
An inland Theskan trade center that supports caravan logistics between frontier routes and Sea of Fallen Stars markets.

## Governance and Power
- Governed through merchant councils, caravan captains, and contracted civic officers.
- Gate and road security commands hold outsized influence during peak trade seasons.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Caravan Yards: stables, teamster compounds, and guarded freight courts.
- Ledger Square: brokers, moneychangers, and arbitration halls.
- East Road Fort: fortified checkpoint controlling outbound overland traffic.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Caravan staging, resupply contracts, and dispute arbitration drive local wealth.
- Culture trends practical and multilingual due to constant traveler turnover.

## Adventure Use
- Resolve a violent dispute over forged caravan credentials.
- Scout a disrupted eastern road before a critical convoy departs.
- Protect arbitrators deciding a high-value trade liability case.

## Canon Notes
Precise district naming and political structure are intentionally broad to support varying table canon.
