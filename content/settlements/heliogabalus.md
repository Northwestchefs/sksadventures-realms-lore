# Heliogabalus

## Overview
A fortified inland city in [[nations-realms/damara|Damara]] that anchors trade roads and serves as a defensive choke point between settled valleys and frontier routes.

## Governance and Power
- A local governor administers taxation, justice, and defense under royal mandate.
- Merchant syndics and knightly orders influence policy through convoy funding and security commitments.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Gate Bastions: heavily fortified city approaches controlling toll and entry.
- Caravan Ward: warehouses, counting houses, and teamster inns.
- Chapel Ring: clustered shrines where temples coordinate charity, records, and militia support.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Revenue depends on caravan tolls, grain exchange, and metalwork tied to frontier demand.
- Citizens value practical faith, disciplined watch service, and mutual aid during siege scares.

## Adventure Use
- Defend a caravan conclave from sabotage aimed at collapsing cross-border trade.
- Investigate disappearances along a tax road used by royal auditors.
- Mediate a feud between a knightly commandery and warehouse consortium before violence erupts.

## Canon Notes
Chronology and naming emphasis differ across editions; present Heliogabalus as a strategic Damaran stronghold with adjustable local detail.
