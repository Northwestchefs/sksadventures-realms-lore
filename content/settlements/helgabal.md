# Helgabal

## Overview
A royal center in [[nations-realms/damara|Damara]] that functions as both court city and military logistics hub for northern frontier defense.

## Governance and Power
- Crown ministers, noble envoys, and military officers compete for influence around the royal bureaucracy.
- Temple representatives hold visible authority in relief efforts, legitimacy rituals, and wartime mobilization.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Crown Hill: palace complex, court chambers, and administrative offices.
- Muster Yards: barracks and staging grounds for border patrols and levy musters.
- Wintermarket: walled trade district where caravan masters and guild brokers negotiate seasonal prices.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- The city economy blends court spending, overland caravan trade, and military provisioning contracts.
- Local culture prizes endurance, oathkeeping, and practical alliances during harsh winters.

## Adventure Use
- Escort negotiators through rival noble districts before a high-stakes war council.
- Track missing pay chests intended for frontier garrisons.
- Uncover a forged decree used to redirect militia forces away from a vulnerable pass.

## Canon Notes
Sources and eras vary on Damara's seat of power; use Helgabal as a campaign-ready royal hub when needed.
