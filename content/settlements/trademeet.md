# Trademeet

## Overview
Trademeet is a major market settlement in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], known for caravan commerce, local bargaining traditions, and tensions between outside guild pressure and local power brokers.

## Campaign Use
Use Trademeet for caravan arcs, market disputes, labor tension, and adventures where local custom collides with metropolitan interests from [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]].
