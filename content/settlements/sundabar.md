# Sundabar

## Overview
Fortified trade city in [[nations-realms/luruar|Luruar (Silver Marches)]], positioned as a hard-edged industrial and military hub of the confederation.

## Role in the Realm
Sundabar supports confederation security with weapons production, caravan staging, and disciplined garrison culture tied to frontier defense.

## Power and Institutions
City authorities, guild forges, and military leadership share power in a practical coalition focused on stability and readiness.

## Campaign Use
Strong for defense contracts, convoy missions, guild disputes, and investigations tied to arms supply, sabotage, or siege preparation.
