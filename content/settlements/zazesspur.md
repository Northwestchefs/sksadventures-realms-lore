# Zazesspur

## Overview
Zazesspur is a major Tethyrian port where maritime trade, private wealth, and political influence often outpace formal titles.

## Location
On Tethyr's southern coast, it connects local markets to wider shipping networks touching the western Realms.

## Civic Pattern
- merchant houses wielding heavy leverage in city decisions
- harbor authorities and tax offices negotiating control over customs revenue
- noble and civic blocs competing to define "lawful" trade practice

## Pressure Points
- smuggling and tariff manipulation around high-value cargoes
- labor unrest when guild contracts shift under outside pressure
- diplomatic friction with Amnian traders over access, fees, and favoritism

## Campaign Use
Use Zazesspur for dockside investigations, convoy work, merchant intrigue, and stories about how money reshapes local authority.
