# Sarshel

## Overview
A key coastal city in [[nations-realms/impiltur|Impiltur]] known for active defense posture and strong civic-religious institutions.

## Governance and Power
- Local magistrates coordinate with military command and influential temple leadership.
- Regional nobles use Sarshel as a staging center for maritime policy and patrols.

## Districts and Landmarks
- Sea Wall District: fortified waterfront and anti-raid defenses.
- Pilgrim Market: mixed trade quarter serving travelers and temple visitors.
- Watch Citadel: command center for coastal scouting and response forces.

## Economy and Culture Hooks
- Economy relies on coastal trade, fisheries, and military support contracts.
- Civic identity emphasizes vigilance, duty, and public works linked to faith.

## Adventure Use
- Investigate missing scouts after reports of offshore raider activity.
- Guard a convoy carrying temple tithe stores through contested waters.
- Expose corruption in fortification contracts before a seasonal storm surge.

## Canon Notes
Some source material emphasizes different institutional power balances; keep civic vs. temple authority flexible.
