# Skuld

## Overview
Skuld is the capital of [[nations-realms/mulhorand|Mulhorand]], a river-centered imperial city where court and temple authority overlap.

## Role in the Realm
The city anchors restoration policy, tax administration, and military logistics for the Mulhorandi heartland.

## Power and Institutions
Imperial officials, temple hierarchies, and military commanders all hold visible influence in civic decisions.

## Campaign Use
Use Skuld for court petitions, temple diplomacy, and missions tied to border mobilization along Unther and Thay routes.
