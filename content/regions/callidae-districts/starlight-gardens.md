# Starlight Gardens

## Overview
A broad district of cultivated groves and ritual orchards that sustains Callidae's food, medicine, and sacred plant traditions.

## Atmosphere
Soft silver light filters through vine-lattices, with drifting pollen-lanterns and quiet song rites between glowing terraces.
