# Rootmarket

## Overview
Callidae's trade quarter, woven through colossal roots where cavern caravans exchange goods, lore, and favors.

## Atmosphere
Warm lantern fungi, dense bargaining crowds, and rope-lifts creaking between root halls create constant motion.
