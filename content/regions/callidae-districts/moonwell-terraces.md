# Moonwell Terraces

## Overview
A tiered district of sacred pools used for healing, augury, and ceremonial gatherings among Callidae's circles.

## Atmosphere
Cool mist hangs over mirror-still waters while moonstones pulse in slow rhythm with communal chants.
