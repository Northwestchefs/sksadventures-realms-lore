# Halruaa

## Overview
Halruaa is treated here as a nation-scale realm entry for campaign planning, diplomacy, and map-level play.

## Identity
A magocracy-level realm where arcane capability and civic administration are deeply intertwined.

## Geography
A southern realm with difficult approaches and protected heartlands, linked to [[nations-realms/samarach|Samarach]] and [[nations-realms/lapaliiya|Lapaliiya]] routes.

## Government
State power is exercised through arcane institutions, civil bureaucracy, and controlled regional offices.

## Society
Education, magical literacy, and status networks strongly influence opportunity and prestige.

## Economy
Arcane services, high-skill crafts, and controlled long-distance trade underpin wealth.

## Religion
Temples coexist with arcane governance, sometimes in partnership and sometimes in tension.

## Military
Magically supported forces, air or rapid-response assets, and fortified nodes define defense posture.

## Major Settlements
- [[settlements/index|Halarahh]] (detail page TBD)
- [[settlements/index|Azul]] (detail page TBD)

## Factions and Power Blocs
- Arcane academies and senior circles
- Civil ministries and magistrates
- Traditionalists versus reform-minded practitioners

## Relations
Maintains selective diplomacy with nearby southlands and cautious distance from hostile magical powers.

## Threats
- Arcane accidents with realm-scale fallout
- Elite factional deadlock
- External theft of magical knowledge

## Adventure Use
Use Halruaa for border diplomacy, faction bargaining, travel logistics, and region-level crisis arcs.

## Hooks
- A disputed treaty clause pulls the party into negotiations between local power blocs and foreign envoys.
- A key route node goes silent, and multiple factions race to control what happened there.
- A local authority hires neutral troubleshooters before a political summit turns violent.
