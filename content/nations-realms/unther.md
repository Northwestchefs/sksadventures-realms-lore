# Unther

## Overview
Unther is treated here as a nation-scale realm entry for campaign planning, diplomacy, and map-level play.

## Identity
A legacy imperial polity in flux, still treated as a major realm-scale actor in regional politics.

## Geography
Lower river and coastal lands adjoining [[nations-realms/mulhorand|Mulhorand]] and [[nations-realms/chessenta|Chessenta]].

## Government
Authority alternates between imperial restoration efforts, military governance, and regional powerholders by era.

## Society
Identity is shaped by imperial memory, displacement, and contested legitimacy narratives.

## Economy
River agriculture, reconstruction labor, and strategic trade corridors drive recovery.

## Religion
State religion and temple politics remain central to sovereignty debates.

## Military
Forces prioritize border holding, city control, and deterrence against stronger neighbors.

## Major Settlements
- [[settlements/index|Unthalass]] (detail page TBD)
- [[settlements/index|Messemprar]] (detail page TBD)

## Factions and Power Blocs
- Imperial loyalists and restorationists
- Provincial military commands
- Temple hierarchies with rival claims

## Relations
Maintains high-stakes relations with [[nations-realms/mulhorand|Mulhorand]] and variable ties with [[nations-realms/chessenta|Chessenta]].

## Threats
- Renewed interstate war
- Internal legitimacy crises
- Economic collapse from prolonged mobilization

## Adventure Use
Use Unther for border diplomacy, faction bargaining, travel logistics, and region-level crisis arcs.

## Hooks
- A disputed treaty clause pulls the party into negotiations between local power blocs and foreign envoys.
- A key route node goes silent, and multiple factions race to control what happened there.
- A local authority hires neutral troubleshooters before a political summit turns violent.
