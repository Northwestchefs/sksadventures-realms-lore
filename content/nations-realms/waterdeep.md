# Waterdeep

## Overview
Waterdeep is a major city-state power whose wealth, diplomacy, and information networks shape politics far beyond its walls.

## Identity
Often called the City of Splendors, Waterdeep blends commercial ambition, cosmopolitan culture, and tightly managed elite governance.

## Geography
Positioned on the Sword Coast along crucial sea and overland routes, Waterdeep acts as a hinge between northern frontiers and southern markets.

## Government
Power is exercised through civic institutions, masked leadership traditions, and influential noble and mercantile circles.

## Society
Waterdeep is socially layered but highly mobile by Realms standards, attracting migrants, adventurers, scholars, and speculators.

## Economy
Shipping, finance, skilled craft production, and long-distance trade make Waterdeep one of the strongest economic engines on the coast.

## Religion
Many faiths maintain recognized urban presence, with temples serving as both spiritual centers and civic actors.

## Military
City watch structures, naval defense, hired specialists, and allied adventuring talent all contribute to security.

## Major Settlements
- [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]]: political, financial, and cultural center of the polity.

## Factions and Power Blocs
- [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]]
- masked civic leadership circles
- major merchant costers and guild alliances
- noble houses with district-level influence, including [[noble-houses/house-cassalanter|House Cassalanter]]
- covert networks competing over information control

## Relations
Waterdeep maintains broad diplomatic ties, balancing trade pragmatism with strategic caution toward expansionist neighbors and predatory powers.

## Threats
- elite factional deadlock
- corruption in licensing, tariffs, and contracts
- espionage and sabotage targeting port logistics
- undercity and hinterland threats spilling into urban politics

## Adventure Use
Waterdeep supports urban intrigue, faction arbitration, investigation campaigns, mercantile competition, and high-level diplomatic missions.

## Hooks
- A shipping registry is altered overnight, redirecting legal cargo into a smuggling seizure trap.
- Rival patrons each claim the party uncovered proof of civic corruption and demand exclusive control of it.
- A district security crisis reveals a deeper struggle between lawful authority and deniable private enforcers.
