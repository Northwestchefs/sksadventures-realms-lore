# Neverwinter

## Overview
Neverwinter is a northern city-state polity known for resilience, reconstruction cycles, and strategic influence along frontier trade routes.

## Identity
The realm blends aspirational civic pride with recurring pressure from external threats and internal rebuilding politics.

## Geography
Its coastal position and river access make Neverwinter a key connector between maritime trade and inland movement through the North.

## Government
Leadership typically centers on a ruling lordship supported by civic officials, military command, and merchant stakeholders.

## Society
Residents are often defined by practical solidarity: veterans, craftspeople, traders, and newcomers rebuilding opportunity after crises.

## Economy
Ship traffic, frontier exchange, reconstruction labor, and artisan industries underpin local prosperity.

## Religion
Multiple temples support civic life, charity, and social stability, especially during post-conflict recovery periods.

## Military
City defense combines standing forces, contracted specialists, and alliance coordination with nearby powers.

## Major Settlements
- [[settlements/neverwinter-city|Neverwinter (City)]]: political and economic center.

## Factions and Power Blocs
- ruling court and civic administration
- merchant and guild reconstruction coalitions
- security command structures and veteran networks
- regional agents competing for influence in the North

## Relations
Neverwinter balances cooperation and rivalry with other Sword Coast powers while guarding its autonomy and trade access.

## Threats
- reconstruction corruption and contract fraud
- frontier incursions and sabotage
- succession disputes under external pressure
- resource shortages during high migration periods

## Adventure Use
Strong for frontier politics, city rebuilding campaigns, anti-corruption arcs, and defensive operations.

## Hooks
- A reconstruction fund vanishes, and multiple officials point to different scapegoats.
- A frontier convoy route collapses under coordinated raids from unknown backers.
- The party is asked to investigate a public works project hiding military smuggling.
