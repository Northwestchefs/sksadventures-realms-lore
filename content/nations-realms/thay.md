# Thay

## Overview
Thay is a feared arcane power whose state structures are shaped by magical authority and coercive control.

## Identity
A realm associated with disciplined magical hierarchy, ruthless ambition, and external manipulation.

## Geography
Located in the eastern lands with strategically significant approaches and defensible interior zones.

## Government
Rule centers on arcane elites and tightly controlled administrative machinery.

## Society
Social rank, personal security, and opportunity are deeply influenced by proximity to power.

## Economy
State-directed extraction, magical production, and controlled trade channels sustain national strength.
