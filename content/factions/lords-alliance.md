# Lords' Alliance

## Overview
A diplomatic and security alliance of major northern and western powers focused on stability, trade safety, and coordinated response to threats.

## Goals
- Keep major routes and ports open
- Prevent regional destabilization by warlords, raiders, and hostile powers
- Coordinate intelligence and limited joint action between member states

## Areas of Influence
Strongest around the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]] and connected trade corridors.

## Typical Tactics
Treaties, emissary networks, shared alerts, joint patrols, and deniable contractors.

## Key Ties
- [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]]
- [[nations-realms/neverwinter|Neverwinter]]
- [[nations-realms/baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]]
- [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]]

## Campaign Use
Best for escort mandates, intercity diplomacy, and missions where lawful priorities conflict with local realities.
