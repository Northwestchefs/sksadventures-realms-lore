# Red Wizards of Thay

## Overview

A powerful arcane elite network associated with [[nations-realms/thay|Thay]] and its magocratic state traditions.

## Goals

- Protect and expand Thayan magical supremacy
- Control dangerous artifacts, necromantic research, and ritual sites
- Influence foreign trade and politics through enclaves and proxies

## Methods and Reach

Cells, emissaries, and sponsored intermediaries can appear in distant markets, courts, and clandestine operations.
