# Emerald Enclave

## Overview

A loose network of druids, rangers, scouts, and village wardens that protects the natural balance and resists threats that scar the land.

## Goals

- Keep frontier expansion from becoming ecological collapse
- Stop blights, unnatural predators, and destructive magic
- Maintain practical peace between settlements and nearby wilds

## Areas of Influence

Forests, marshes, old growth routes, and river systems near the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]], [[regions/western-heartlands|Western Heartlands]], and adjacent frontier zones.

## Typical Tactics

Local guides, covert observation, beast-handling, sabotage of destructive logging/mining operations, and targeted strikes on corrupt ritual sites.
