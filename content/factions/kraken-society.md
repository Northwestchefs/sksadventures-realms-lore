# Kraken Society

## Overview
A clandestine sea-cult network that manipulates captains, port officials, and smugglers through fear, prophecy, and blackmail.

## Goals
- Destabilize coastal powers through covert maritime shocks
- Co-opt pirate fleets and shipping guild officers
- Gather relics tied to deep-sea entities and storm rites

## Areas of Influence
- pirate corridors around the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]]
- hidden agents in [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]] and [[nations-realms/moonshae-isles|Moonshae Isles]]
- ritual caches in remote coves and lighthouse ruins

## Typical Tactics
False-flag raids, coded signal chains, hostage pressure, and occult terror theater.
