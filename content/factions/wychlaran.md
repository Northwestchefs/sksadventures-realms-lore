# Wychlaran

## Overview
The Wychlaran are the spirit-guided witch circles of [[nations-realms/rashemen|Rashemen]], deeply tied to law, memory, and homeland defense.

## Goals
- Guard Rashemen from foreign magical domination
- Preserve clan traditions and spiritual covenants
- Guide rulers and war leaders through omen and counsel

## Areas of Influence
- ritual sites and council circles in [[settlements/immilmar|Immilmar]]
- border-watch posts facing [[nations-realms/thay|Thay]]
- clan territories throughout eastern forests and steppe routes

## Typical Tactics
Divination, spirit compacts, weather-working, and selective deployment of berserker allies.
