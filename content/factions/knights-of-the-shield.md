# Knights of the Shield

## Overview
A hidden society of financiers, smugglers, and influence brokers who weaponize trade secrets and noble debts.

## Goals
- Place agents inside customs houses, counting houses, and courts
- Convert financial crises into political leverage
- Keep faction identities deniable behind shell ventures

## Areas of Influence
- [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]] and major caravan finance routes
- elite circles in [[nations-realms/sembia|Sembia]]
- quiet partnerships in port cities across the [[regions/western-heartlands|Western Heartlands]]

## Typical Tactics
Blackmail ledgers, forged trade warrants, controlled defaults, and contract assassinations.
