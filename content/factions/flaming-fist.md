# Flaming Fist

## Overview
The premier armed force of [[nations-realms/baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]], balancing civic defense, paid enforcement, and political pressure.

## Goals
- Defend Baldurian trade interests on roads and riverways
- Suppress unrest before it threatens elite revenue
- Project city influence through mercenary contracts

## Areas of Influence
- [[settlements/baldurs-gate-city|Baldur's Gate (City)]] districts and gate approaches
- convoy routes along the [[regions/trade-way|Trade Way]]
- occasional operations toward the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]] frontier

## Typical Tactics
Disciplined patrols, checkpoint control, deputized adventurers, and rapid punitive strikes.
