# Cowled Wizards

## Overview

The Cowled Wizards are an arcane regulatory power strongly associated with [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]] and the wider political order of [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]].

## Public Role

- Supervise or restrict unsanctioned spell use in key urban areas
- Provide magical services to elite patrons and civic authorities
- Act as both security institution and political leverage point

## Notable NPCs

- [[people/ellis-fenward|Ellis Fenward]]

## Campaign Use

Use the Cowled Wizards for licensing disputes, magical investigations, anti-mage crackdowns, and faction bargaining over who gets legal arcane access.

## Canon Notes

Portray exact legal authority and methods by era; sources differ on how centralized and severe enforcement is.
