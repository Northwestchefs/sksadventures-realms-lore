# Order of the Gauntlet

## Overview

A coalition of zealous knights, templars, and holy champions committed to crushing overt evil across the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]] and beyond.

## Goals

- Destroy cults, undead tyrants, and fiend-backed cells
- Protect vulnerable communities through direct action
- Pressure rulers to respond decisively to existential threats

## Areas of Influence

Most visible on major roads, pilgrimage routes, and frontier settlements where monsters, raiders, or cultists are active.

## Typical Tactics

Public musters, fortified patrol camps, armed escorts, and strike teams against known evil strongholds.
