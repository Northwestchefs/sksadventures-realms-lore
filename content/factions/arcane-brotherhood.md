# Arcane Brotherhood

## Overview
A competitive league of wizards centered on [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]] that treats magic as leverage, currency, and state power.

## Goals
- Secure rare spellcraft, relics, and magical sites before rivals
- Influence port politics through arcane services and intimidation
- Expand Brotherhood cells into high-value trade cities

## Areas of Influence
- [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]] and northern sea routes
- selective towers and counting houses in [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]] and [[nations-realms/baldurs-gate|Baldur's Gate]]
- expedition fronts tied to lost Netherese and giant-era ruins

## Typical Tactics
Contract magic, magical espionage, debt-binding favors, and deniable proxy expeditions.

Use them for magical cold wars, expedition races, and patrons who reward success but never forgive failure.
