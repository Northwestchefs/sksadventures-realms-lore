# Bregan D'aerthe

## Overview
A drow mercenary network that trades in information, sabotage, and deniable violence across major ports.

## Goals
- Profit from faction wars without becoming trapped in them
- Place deep-cover assets in dock guilds and noble households
- Secure sea-lane intelligence before rival syndicates

## Areas of Influence
- covert cells in [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]] and [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]]
- smuggling interfaces along the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]]
- liaison channels with Underdark intermediaries

## Typical Tactics
Identity masks, layered aliases, debt leverage, and rapid extraction teams.
