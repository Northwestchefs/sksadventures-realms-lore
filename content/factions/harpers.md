# Harpers

## Overview

A loose alliance of agents, scholars, and troubleshooters that opposes tyranny and hidden corruption across many realms.

## Goals

- Disrupt authoritarian overreach and predatory power blocs
- Protect communities from abuses hidden behind official institutions
- Share information to counter escalating regional threats

## Areas of Influence

Distributed cells across major cities, courts, and travel routes rather than formal territorial control.

## Typical Tactics

Information brokering, covert investigations, discreet aid, and limited deniable operations.
