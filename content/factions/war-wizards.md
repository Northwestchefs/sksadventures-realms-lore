# War Wizards

## Overview
A crown-aligned arcane institution in [[nations-realms/cormyr|Cormyr]] focused on state security and magical oversight.

## Methods and Reach
War Wizards are commonly associated with intelligence support, magical countermeasures, and high-trust royal assignments.

## Campaign Use
Useful for court missions, espionage counterplay, artifact containment, and politically sensitive investigations.
