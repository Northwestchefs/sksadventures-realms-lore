# War Wizards

## Overview

A crown-aligned arcane institution in [[nations-realms/cormyr|Cormyr]] focused on state security and magical oversight.

## Mandate

- Counter magical threats to crown, court, and military command
- Vet high-risk mages and arcane artifacts tied to state security
- Support royal intelligence with divination and counter-intelligence

## Methods and Reach

War Wizards are associated with intelligence support, magical countermeasures, and high-trust royal assignments.
