# Cult of the Dragon

## Overview

A secretive draconic cult network pursuing long-term power through relics, agents, and alliances with dangerous patrons.

## Goals

- Expand draconic influence over trade, fear, and tribute routes.
- Recover artifacts and texts tied to dragon prophecy.
- Infiltrate local elites through bribery, blackmail, or false patronage.

## Areas of Influence

Cells can appear in ports, caravan cities, and frontier keeps where wealth and secrecy intersect.

## Typical Tactics

Smuggling fronts, coded correspondence, disposable intermediaries, and ritual sites hidden near contested ruins.
