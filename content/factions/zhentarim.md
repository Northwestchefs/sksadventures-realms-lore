# Zhentarim

## Overview

A flexible power network of brokers, mercenaries, and fixers that mixes legitimate contracts with coercive leverage.

## Goals

- Expand influence over trade corridors and local security markets
- Build deniable control through debt, force, and patronage
- Position intermediaries inside rival civic and mercantile institutions

## Areas of Influence

Strongest where law enforcement is fragmented, border routes are contested, or elites need deniable specialists.

## Typical Tactics

Protection contracts, intelligence brokering, proxy fronts, debt pressure, and selective violence.
