# House de'Lyr

## Overview
House de'Lyr is presented as a major mercantile-noble line in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]], with influence in finance, shipping, and contract politics. The house is best used as a campaign placeholder for elite patronage and intra-oligarch rivalry.

## Campaign Use
Use House de'Lyr as patrons, financiers, antagonists, or political kingmakers whose interests intersect with the [[factions/council-of-six|Council of Six]].
