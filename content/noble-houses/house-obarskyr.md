# House Obarskyr

## Overview
House Obarskyr is the ruling royal line of [[nations-realms/cormyr|Cormyr]] in many campaign presentations.

## Sphere of Influence
Its authority is strongest through crown institutions, military command legitimacy, and court patronage networks.

## Campaign Use
Useful for succession tension, royal diplomacy, and conflicts between state duty and personal allegiance.
