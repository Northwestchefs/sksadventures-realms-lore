# Krenlin Auvryndar

## Overview
A polished surface-identity broker for [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]] who trades masks, passports, and selective betrayals.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Intelligence broker and covert facilitator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/bregan-daerthe|Bregan D'aerthe]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Intermittent deals with [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] crews.
- **Base of Operations:** Safehouses between [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]] and [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Dresses in understated noble fabrics that never draw attention twice.
- Keeps small cards with rotating identities in a silver case.

## History / Background
- Entered surface operations as a teen courier and never returned to a single permanent name.
- Built influence by arranging "impossible" introductions between rivals.
