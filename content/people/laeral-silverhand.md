# Laeral Silverhand

## Overview
Open Lord of [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep]], known for balancing noble interests, guild power, and regional diplomacy across the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]].

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Civic ruler and senior political decision-maker.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]].
- **Secondary Ties:** [[factions/lords-alliance|Lords' Alliance]], informal alignment with [[factions/harpers|Harpers]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
