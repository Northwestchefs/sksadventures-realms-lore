# Toramir Glimmerthorn

## Overview
Toramir Glimmerthorn is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected fungal alchemist among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Fungal Alchemist serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Patchwork lab coat, mushroom-spore mask, and glowing satchel jars.

## Personality
Inventive, impatient, and surprisingly kind.

## Relationships
- [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]]
- [[regions/callidae-districts/rootmarket|Rootmarket]]
- [[people/sereia-dawnpetal|Sereia Dawnpetal]]

## Plot Hooks
