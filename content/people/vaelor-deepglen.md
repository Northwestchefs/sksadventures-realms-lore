# Vaelor Deepglen

## Overview
Vaelor Deepglen is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected cavern ranger among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Cavern Ranger serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Layered leather lamellar and a crystal-tipped spear for tunnel defense.

## Personality
Disciplined, terse, and duty-first.
