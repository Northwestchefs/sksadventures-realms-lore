# Selene Brightmyr

## Overview
Selene Brightmyr is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected star astrologer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Star Astrologer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Flowing midnight-blue robes and a headband of rotating star pins.

## Personality
Warm, visionary, and prone to speaking in metaphor.
