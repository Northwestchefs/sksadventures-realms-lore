# Captain Harlon Drex

## Overview
A veteran convoy commander who sells disciplined escort contracts to merchants unwilling to trust either pirates or corrupt officials.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Mercenary captain and convoy security broker.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Contract law and paid oaths.
- **Secondary Ties:** Working alignment with [[factions/lords-alliance|Lords' Alliance]] monitors; tactical respect for [[people/brother-kestrel-vane|Brother Kestrel Vane]].
- **Base of Operations:** Ironwake Barracks, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Broad-shouldered, gray beard trimmed to regulation, always wearing a weathered contract tube.
- Speaks in short terms: price, risk, route, consequence.

## History / Background
- Former marine officer who left official service after a command dispute over civilian casualties.
- Built a neutral reputation by honoring contracts even when factions demand favoritism.
