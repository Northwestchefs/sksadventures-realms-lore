# Ontharr Frume

## Overview

A prominent field commander of the [[factions/order-of-the-gauntlet|Order of the Gauntlet]], useful when campaigns need a bold anti-cult champion.

## Role / Allegiance

- **Role:** Militant commander and rallying speaker.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/order-of-the-gauntlet|Order of the Gauntlet]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Faith-militant circles tied to [[faiths/tempus|Tempus]] and cooperation with [[factions/harpers|Harpers]].
- **Base of Operations:** Mobile camps across the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]].
