# Theron Deepstar

## Overview
Theron Deepstar is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected cavern ranger among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Cavern Ranger serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Heavy boots, tunnel chalks, and a shield inlaid with star-glass.

## Personality
Steadfast, blunt, and community-minded.
