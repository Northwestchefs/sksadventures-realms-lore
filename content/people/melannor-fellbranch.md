# Melannor Fellbranch

## Overview

A seasoned warden tied to the [[factions/emerald-enclave|Emerald Enclave]], useful for balancing settlement needs against wilderness stability.

## Role / Allegiance

- **Role:** Druidic scoutmaster and regional advisor.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/emerald-enclave|Emerald Enclave]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Occasional cooperation with [[factions/harpers|Harpers]] and local rangers near [[nations-realms/neverwinter|Neverwinter]].
- **Base of Operations:** Wildlands outside [[settlements/neverwinter-city|Neverwinter (City)]].
