# Fenlor Starwatch

## Overview
Fenlor Starwatch is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected star astrologer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Star Astrologer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
High-collared robe and brass astrolabe harness over one shoulder.

## Personality
Measured, skeptical, and precise with language.
