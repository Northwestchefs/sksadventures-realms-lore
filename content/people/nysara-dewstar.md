# Nysara Dewstar

## Overview
Nysara Dewstar is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected moonwell healer among the Starlight Elves.

Moonwell Healer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Simple white wraps, silver basin tattoos, and clear quartz charms.
