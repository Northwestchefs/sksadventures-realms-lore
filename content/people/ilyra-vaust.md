# Ilyra Vaust

## Overview
A razor-calm expedition broker for the [[factions/arcane-brotherhood|Arcane Brotherhood]] who funds ruin delves and then bills survivors for "arcane risk adjustment."

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Arcane quartermaster and expedition patron.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/arcane-brotherhood|Arcane Brotherhood]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Transactional channels with [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] fixers.
- **Base of Operations:** A warded counting room near [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Keeps immaculate ink-stained gloves and never signs the same way twice.
- Speaks politely, but always as if drafting leverage.

## History / Background
- Began as a copyist for dockside spell contracts and rose by exposing forged spell warrants.
- Built a private ledger of favors owed by captains, mages, and smugglers.
