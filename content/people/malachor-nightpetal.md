# Malachor Nightpetal

## Overview
Malachor Nightpetal is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected magical researcher among the Starlight Elves.

## Description
Long coat with warding runes, and crystal vials along his belt.
