# Kaelion Rootflare

## Overview
Kaelion Rootflare is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected rootmarket broker among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Rootmarket Broker serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Polished boots, trade ledgers on chain clips, and amber earrings.

## Personality
Fast-talking, opportunistic, and surprisingly loyal to friends.
