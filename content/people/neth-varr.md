# Neth Varr

## Overview
A storm-priest agitator tied to the [[factions/kraken-society|Kraken Society]] who can turn frightened dockworkers into a riot overnight.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Cult recruiter and maritime saboteur.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/kraken-society|Kraken Society]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Pirate intermediaries near the Moonshaes.
- **Base of Operations:** Rotating shrine-caches on isolated Moonshae coves.

## Description
- Salt-white hair braided with shark teeth and tarnished bronze charms.
- Eyes reflect lanternlight like wet black glass.

## History / Background
- Former harbor chaplain who vanished after a disastrous storm season and returned preaching "necessary drownings."
- Built cult cells by predicting ship losses and offering survivors someone to blame.
