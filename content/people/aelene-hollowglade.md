# Aelene Hollowglade

## Overview
Aelene Hollowglade is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected cavern ranger among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Cavern Ranger serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Dark green scout cloak and a carved bow of moonroot wood.

## Personality
Cool-headed, pragmatic, and quietly compassionate.
