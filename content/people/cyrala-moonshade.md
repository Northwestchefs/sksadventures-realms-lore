# Cyrala Moonshade

## Overview
Cyrala Moonshade is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected ancient druid among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Ancient Druid serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Stooped but imposing, with a moonwood staff and rings of seed-amber.

## Personality
Quiet, wise, and occasionally cryptic.
