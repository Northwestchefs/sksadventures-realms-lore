# Miralys Starwell

## Overview
Miralys Starwell is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected moonwell priestess among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Moonwell Priestess serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Moon-pale veil, basin tattoos, and luminous pearl rosary beads.

## Personality
Serene, empathetic, and uncompromising in sacred matters.
