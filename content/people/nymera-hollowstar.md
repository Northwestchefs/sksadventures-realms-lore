# Nymera Hollowstar

## Overview
Nymera Hollowstar is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected faerie diplomat among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Faerie Diplomat serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
She dresses in dew-bright fabrics and wears living vine jewelry.

## Personality
Graceful, diplomatic, and quietly iron-willed.
