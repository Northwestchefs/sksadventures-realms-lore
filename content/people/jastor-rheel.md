# Jastor Rheel

## Overview
A velvet-voiced commodity banker who secretly serves the [[factions/knights-of-the-shield|Knights of the Shield]] by engineering profitable crises.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Merchant financier and covert blackmail broker.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/knights-of-the-shield|Knights of the Shield]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Quiet trade with [[factions/shadow-thieves-athkatla|Shadow Thieves (Athkatla)]].
- **Base of Operations:** Rotating offices between [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]] and [[settlements/selgaunt|Selgaunt]].

## Description
- Impeccable coats with hidden blade seams and scent of expensive myrrh ink.
- Smiles as if every conversation already has collateral attached.

## History / Background
- Started as a dock auditor, then leveraged corruption files to buy entry into elite trade circles.
- Became valuable to conspirators by turning scandal into debt instruments.
