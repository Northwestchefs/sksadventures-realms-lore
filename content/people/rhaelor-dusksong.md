# Rhaelor Dusksong

## Overview
Rhaelor Dusksong is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected scout captain among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Scout Captain serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Lean frame, scarred jawline, and a cloak that blends with cave shadow.

## Personality
Protective, decisive, and sometimes overly suspicious.
