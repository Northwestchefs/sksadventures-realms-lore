# Talren Mycelthorn

## Overview
Talren Mycelthorn is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected fungal alchemist among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Fungal Alchemist serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
A stout elder with white brows, knotwood cane, and purple spore pouches.

## Personality
Patient, sardonic, and fond of teaching apprentices.
