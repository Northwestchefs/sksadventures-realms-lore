# Relae Starpetal

## Overview
Relae Starpetal is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected crystal artisan among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Crystal Artisan serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Glittering cuffs, translucent gloves, and a rotating shard halo.

## Personality
Expressive, perfectionist, and generous with apprentices.
