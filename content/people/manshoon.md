# Manshoon

## Overview

A feared archmage identity associated with hardline [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] power plays, clone intrigues, and covert domination campaigns.

## Role / Allegiance

- **Role:** Arcane strategist and faction usurper.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Hardline cells of the [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Shadow conflicts with [[factions/harpers|Harpers]] and urban elites in [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]].
- **Base of Operations:** Hidden enclaves along the [[regions/sword-coast|Sword Coast]].
