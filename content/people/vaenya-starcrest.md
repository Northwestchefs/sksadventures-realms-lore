# Vaenya Starcrest

## Overview
Vaenya Starcrest is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected rootmarket merchant among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Rootmarket Merchant serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Layered trader robes, gemstone scales, and coin-strand braids.

## Personality
Confident, fair-minded, and hard to deceive.
