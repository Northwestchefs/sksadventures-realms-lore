# Sereia Dawnpetal

## Overview
An ambitious bloom-caller in [[regions/callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]] known for controlled experiments that blend lunar cultivation rites with living crystal horticulture.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Experimental cultivator and greenhouse lead.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Sapmoon research circle in [[landmarks/sapmoon-greenhouse|Sapmoon Greenhouse]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Healing delegates from [[regions/callidae-districts/moonwell-terraces|Moonwell Terraces]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[landmarks/sapmoon-greenhouse|Sapmoon Greenhouse]].

## Appearance
- Wears layered greenhouse veils stitched with reflective pollen-thread and crystal seed clasps.
- Speaks quickly, sketches graft patterns mid-conversation, and keeps luminescent sap stains on her gloves.

## Description
- Warm and collaborative with healers, impatient with bureaucracy, and willing to take calculated risks.
- Frames every debate around how many lives faster cultivation could save.

## History / Background
- Rose quickly after preventing a terrace-wide rot by designing an emergency cross-graft protocol.
- Her methods are praised in crisis and criticized during stable seasons.
