# Vesk Talandra

## Overview
Chief night constable for Luskan's dock wards, balancing fragile order with thin manpower and constant political interference.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Watch commander and crisis response coordinator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]] civic order.
- **Secondary Ties:** Information-sharing with [[people/thalia-brineledger|Thalia Brineledger]] and reluctant cooperation with [[people/ellis-fenward|Ellis Fenward]].
- **Base of Operations:** Northward Watchhouse, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Keeps dark hair cropped close for helmet fit and wears a whistle carved from whale bone.
- Known for direct questions and exact memory of who was where at what bell.

## History / Background
- Promoted after surviving a dock riot and organizing civilian evacuation under fire.
- Her authority is practical but politically fragile; captains respect results more than warrants.
