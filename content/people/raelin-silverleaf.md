# Raelin Silverleaf

## Overview
A half-elf outrider used by caravan masters when roads turn political and dangerous.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Trail scout and quiet negotiator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Independent caravan contracts.
- **Secondary Ties:** Friendly with [[factions/harpers|Harpers]] couriers.
- **Base of Operations:** [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].

## Appearance
- Weather cloak, white ash bow, silver-threaded gloves.
- Keeps route maps in waxed leather rolls.
