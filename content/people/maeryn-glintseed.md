# Maeryn Glintseed

## Overview
Maeryn Glintseed is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected fungal alchemist among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Fungal Alchemist serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Short and quick, with amber goggles and satchels of labeled spores.
