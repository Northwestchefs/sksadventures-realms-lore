# Irielle Fernglow

## Overview
Irielle Fernglow is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected druid gardener among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Druid Gardener serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Leaf-stitched apron, crescent pruning knife, and luminous pollen braids.

## Personality
