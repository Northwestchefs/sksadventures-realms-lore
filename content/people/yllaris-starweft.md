# Yllaris Starweft

## Overview
Yllaris Starweft is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected magical researcher among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Magical Researcher serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Arcane gloves etched with runes and a cloak lined in silver thread.

## Personality
Brilliant, intense, and distractible.
