# Elaria Starwhisper

## Overview
Elaria Starwhisper is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected star astrologer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Star Astrologer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Night-silk robe and star charts tattooed across her forearms.

## Personality
Dreamy, insightful, and quietly authoritative.
