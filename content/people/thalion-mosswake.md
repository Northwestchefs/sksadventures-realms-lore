# Thalion Mosswake

## Overview
A senior seed-warden in [[regions/callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]] who enforces cultivation law, quarantine lines, and long-cycle crop planning for [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]].

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Seed-warden and registry adjudicator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Keepers of Seeds in [[regions/callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Conservative elder blocs in [[regions/callidae-districts/eldergrove|Eldergrove]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[landmarks/keeper-of-seeds-pavilion|Keeper of Seeds Pavilion]].

## Appearance
- Tall starlight elf with bark-dyed robes, resin-sealed ledgers, and silver pruning hooks.
- Carries a faint scent of cedar ash and moonwort tincture.

## Description
- Speaks in measured, procedural language and expects documented proof before action.
- Rewards discipline and transparency, but becomes uncompromising when quarantine rules are breached.

## History / Background
- Served through multiple blight scares and gained influence by preserving seed lines during lean years.
- Publicly avoids faction rhetoric, yet is widely treated as the face of preservation policy.
