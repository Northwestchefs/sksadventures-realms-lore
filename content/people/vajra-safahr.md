# Vajra Safahr

## Overview

The Blackstaff of [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]], a decisive arcane authority useful for city defense, magical crises, and high-level political pressure.

## Role / Allegiance

- **Role:** Arcane defender and civic crisis responder.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Operational overlap with the [[factions/lords-alliance|Lords' Alliance]] and selective cooperation with [[factions/war-wizards|War Wizards]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
