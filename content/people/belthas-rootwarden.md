# Belthas Rootwarden

## Overview
Belthas Rootwarden is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected druid gardener among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Druid Gardener serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Thick root-brace, bark mantle, and seed-charms hanging from his belt.

## Personality
Reliable, conservative, and deeply loyal to tradition.
