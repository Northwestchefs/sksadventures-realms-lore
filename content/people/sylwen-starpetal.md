# Sylwen Starpetal

## Overview
Sylwen Starpetal is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected moonwell priestess among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Moonwell Priestess serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
She wears layered moon-silk and carries crystal bowls that glow in prayer light.

## Personality
Compassionate, observant, and firm when rituals are ignored.
