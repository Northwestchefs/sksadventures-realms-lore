# Sylara Nightbloom

## Overview
Sylara Nightbloom is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected astral cartographer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Astral Cartographer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Short hair with silver beads and a foldable prism chart board.
