# Renal Bloodscalp

## Overview
A major underworld broker in [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]], useful for campaigns where commerce, organized crime, and civic authority collide in [[nations-realms/amn|Amn]].

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Criminal coordinator and information broker.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/shadow-thieves-athkatla|Shadow Thieves (Athkatla)]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Smuggling networks along the [[regions/amnian-coast|Amnian Coast]], pressure against the [[factions/council-of-six|Council of Six]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[settlements/athkatla|Athkatla]].

- Well-dressed and understated, projecting calm control.
- Avoids overt threats; prefers leverage, debt, and implied consequences.
