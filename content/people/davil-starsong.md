# Davil Starsong

## Overview

A polished fixer associated with [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] operations in [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]], often used for high-risk contracts with plausible deniability.

## Role / Allegiance

- **Role:** Broker, recruiter, and urban operations manager.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Tavern and mercenary circles around [[nations-realms/waterdeep|Waterdeep]].
- **Base of Operations:** [[settlements/waterdeep-city|Waterdeep (City)]].
