# Daerion Moonspire

## Overview
Daerion Moonspire is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected astral observatory scholar among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Astral Observatory Scholar serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Tall with narrow spectacles and layered note-scrolls tucked in his sash.

## Personality
Inquisitive, humble, and idealistic.
