# Marshal Saelra Vonn

## Overview
A hard-driving field commander in the [[factions/flaming-fist|Flaming Fist]] who believes swift order prevents bloodier chaos later.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Flaming Fist district marshal and convoy commander.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/flaming-fist|Flaming Fist]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Coordination with [[factions/lords-alliance|Lords' Alliance]] envoys.
- **Base of Operations:** Wyrm's Crossing barracks, [[settlements/baldurs-gate-city|Baldur's Gate (City)]].

## Description
- Cropped black hair, scarred chin, and bright red gauntlet lacing over practical armor.
- Carries multiple seal-rings to authorize emergency action in different districts.

## History / Background
- Rose from gate patrol to command by surviving two dock riots without letting either become a massacre.
- Earned enemies among smugglers and nobles by prosecuting both with equal force.
