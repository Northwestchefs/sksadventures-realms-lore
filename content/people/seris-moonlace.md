# Seris Moonlace

## Overview
Seris Moonlace is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected astral cartographer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Astral Cartographer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Braided white hair, silver compass tattoos, and a prism monocle.

## Personality
Direct, adventurous, and skeptical of superstition.
