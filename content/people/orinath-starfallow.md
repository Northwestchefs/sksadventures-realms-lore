# Orinath Starfallow

## Overview
Orinath Starfallow is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected ancient druid among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Ancient Druid serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Weathered face, moonbranch staff, and cloak threaded with old ward sigils.

## Personality
Stoic, strategic, and deeply protective of Callidae.
