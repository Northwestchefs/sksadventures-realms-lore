# Brother Kestrel Vane

## Overview
Battle-cleric of [[faiths/tempus|Tempus]] who blesses sailors, buries the drowned, and mediates vendettas before they ignite dockside wars.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Shrine warden, battlefield healer, and neutral mediator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[faiths/tempus|Tempus]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Trusted by [[people/captain-harlon-drex|Captain Harlon Drex]] crews and watched by [[people/vesk-talandra|Vesk Talandra]]'s constables.
- **Base of Operations:** Red Banner Shrine, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Keeps a scarlet prayer sash wrapped over dented chain.
- Gentle with mourners; uncompromising with oathbreakers.

## History / Background
- Once served as a deck chaplain on privateer ships before establishing a shore shrine for crews caught between law and revenge.
- Maintains neutrality by recording every mediated truce in a public shrine ledger.
