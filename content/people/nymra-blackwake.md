# Nymra Blackwake

## Overview
A salvage captain who runs "lost cargo recovery" along the northern lanes, blending legal contracts with deniable work for stronger patrons.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Smuggling captain and maritime fixer.
- **Primary Allegiance:** Her own crew and debts.
- **Secondary Ties:** Profit-driven contracts with [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] brokers; occasional deals with [[people/thalia-brineledger|Thalia Brineledger]].
- **Base of Operations:** Blackwake Pier, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Salt-black hair in tight braids, weather cloak, and brass finger guards for rope work.
- Laughs easily, bargains hard, and never discusses clients twice.

## History / Background
- Started as a deck runner on convoy ships before taking command after a mutiny-turned-rescue in rough northern waters.
- Her current power comes from controlling who can move cargo quietly when official inspections tighten.
