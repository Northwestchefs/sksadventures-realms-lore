# Theren Lumenroot

## Overview
Theren Lumenroot is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected druid gardener among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Druid Gardener serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Broad-shouldered with bark-bracelets, moss-green braids, and soil-stained gloves.

## Personality
Practical, warm, and stubborn about plant stewardship.
