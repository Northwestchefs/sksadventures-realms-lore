# Aelar Moonbrook

## Overview
Aelar Moonbrook is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected astral cartographer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Astral Cartographer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Lean and long-limbed, with silver-blue skin markings and a cloak stitched with star maps.

## Personality
Calm, patient, and quietly curious about every tunnel branch.
