# Liora Valecrest

## Overview
Liora Valecrest is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected rootmarket merchant among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Rootmarket Merchant serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Elegant traveling coat, abacus charms, and a smile that misses little.

## Personality
Charming, shrewd, and protective of her caravan partners.
