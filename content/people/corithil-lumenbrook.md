# Corithil Lumenbrook

## Overview
Corithil Lumenbrook is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected crystal artisan among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Crystal Artisan serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Broad hands dusted in crystal powder and an apron of mirrored plates.

## Personality
Steady, humble, and detail-obsessed.
