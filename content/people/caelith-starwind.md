# Caelith Starwind

## Overview
Caelith Starwind is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected star astrologer among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Star Astrologer serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Pale robes marked with moving constellations and polished moonstone rings.

## Personality
Ceremonial, thoughtful, and cautious with predictions.
