# Orryn Starbark

## Overview
Orryn Starbark is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected druid gardener among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Druid Gardener serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Heavy root-cloak, antlered staff, and moonfern seeds in every pocket.

## Personality
Gentle with plants, stern with people.
