# Eldrin Mossflare

## Overview
Eldrin Mossflare is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected scout among the Starlight Elves.

Scout serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Light armor with glow-moss netting and twin crescent daggers.
