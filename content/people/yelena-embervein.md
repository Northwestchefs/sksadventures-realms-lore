# Yelena Embervein

## Overview
A young but feared field witch of the [[factions/wychlaran|Wychlaran]] who reads battlefield omens in ash, frost, and birdsong.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Border seer and spirit negotiator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/wychlaran|Wychlaran]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Coordination with Rashemi war hosts.
- **Base of Operations:** Seasonal lodges outside [[settlements/immilmar|Immilmar]].

## Description
- Keeps braided copper threads in her hair as spirit ward knots.
- Her voice drops to a whisper before every important warning.

## History / Background
- Survived a failed Thayan raid as a child and was taken in by a Wychlaran circle.
- Earned status by predicting two invasions weeks before scouts confirmed them.
