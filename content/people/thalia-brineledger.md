# Thalia Brineledger

## Overview
Harbor records superintendent in [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]], known for catching forged manifests and quietly steering dock disputes before they become blood feuds.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Port ledger authority and customs investigator.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[nations-realms/luskan|Luskan]].
- **Secondary Ties:** Contract cooperation with [[factions/lords-alliance|Lords' Alliance]], recurring conflict with [[factions/zhentarim|Zhentarim]] fronts.
- **Base of Operations:** Tide Office, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Ink-stained gloves, seal ring, and a calm voice that rarely changes tone.
- Keeps three coded ledgers: legal cargo, suspicious cargo, and "cargo worth surviving."

## History / Background
- Rose from dock tally clerk to records superintendent after exposing a bribery web tied to northern smuggling routes.
- Built a reputation for practical enforcement: seize less, prove more, and survive local politics.
