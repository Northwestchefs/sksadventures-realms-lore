# Nerisse Glowfern

## Overview
Nerisse Glowfern is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected fungal alchemist among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Fungal Alchemist serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Powder-blue robes, layered satchels, and bioluminescent nail varnish.

## Personality
Inventive, empathetic, and slightly theatrical.
