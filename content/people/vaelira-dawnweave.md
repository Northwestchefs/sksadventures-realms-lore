# Vaelira Dawnweave

## Overview
Vaelira Dawnweave is a female starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected crystal artisan among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Crystal Artisan serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Her sleeves glitter with crystal dust, and tiny floating shards orbit her belt.

## Personality
Focused, exacting, and secretly sentimental about old designs.
