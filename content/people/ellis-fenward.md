# Ellis Fenward

## Overview
Owner of a dockside coffeehouse that doubles as a rumor exchange, debt office, and covert message point for anti-corruption operators.

## Role / Allegiance
- **Role:** Informant broker and social fixer.
- **Primary Allegiance:** [[factions/harpers|Harpers]] cell interests.
- **Secondary Ties:** Cooperative triangle with [[people/thalia-brineledger|Thalia Brineledger]] and [[people/vesk-talandra|Vesk Talandra]] when port violence spikes.
- **Base of Operations:** Lantern Cup House, [[settlements/luskan-city|Luskan (City)]].

## Description
- Immaculate aprons, inked cuffs, and a smile that reads as either welcome or warning.
- Keeps coded chalk marks on table legs to track patron loyalties.

## History / Background
- Former caravan clerk who switched to intelligence work after uncovering extortion embedded in port tariffs.
- Maintains a small Harper line in Luskan focused on exposing predatory contracts rather than open sabotage.
