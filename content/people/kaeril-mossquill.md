# Kaeril Mossquill

## Overview
Kaeril Mossquill is a male starlight elf based in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]], known as a respected lore scholar among the Starlight Elves.

## Role
Lore Scholar serving civic and ritual needs in Callidae.

## Description
Green-ink quills, pocket codices, and a braid clasp shaped like a leaf.

## Personality
