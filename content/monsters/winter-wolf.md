## Encounter Role
- **Role:** Predator / Elite.
- **Suggested Grouping:** Pair with lesser wolves or solo alpha encounter.
- **Primary Terrain:** Frozen forest, mountain pass, winter roads.
- **Faction Pressure (if any):** Sometimes driven toward settlements by cult or druid conflict.

## Table Use
- **Signs:** Frost-rimed tracks and silent livestock panic.
- **Tactics:** Flanks with breath weapon pressure, then isolates stragglers.
- **Negotiation:** Backs off for tribute food or if a stronger predator is nearby.
- **Treasure / Clues:** Cache with torn banners from prior caravan attacks.
