# Many-Threads Bazaar

## Description
Rootmarket's central ring of woven stalls, suspended awnings, and bargaining circles where most visitors first enter district trade.

## Trade Focus
- Everyday goods and travel supplies
- Cloth, ropework, and layered leathers
- Salvage lots from old tunnel routes

## NPCs Commonly Present
- [[people/kaelor-deepbargain|Kaelor Deepbargain]]
- [[people/seris-vell|Seris Vell]]

## Story Hooks
- A row of stalls vanishes overnight, leaving only perfectly measured chalk outlines.
- A haggling ritual is interrupted when a relic lot begins speaking in three voices.
