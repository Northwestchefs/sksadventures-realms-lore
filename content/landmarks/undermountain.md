# Undermountain

## Why It Matters
A vast and infamous dungeon complex tied to regional adventuring culture, urban rumor, and high-risk treasure economies.

## Control and Access
No single actor fully controls it; access is shaped by local authorities, expeditions, and opportunistic factions.

## Campaign Use
Useful for megadungeon delves, artifact recovery, and city-connected danger escalation.
