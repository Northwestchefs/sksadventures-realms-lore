# Caravan Rest of Nine Arches

## Description
A neutral inn and contract house built into nine load-bearing roots, each arch reserved for a different caravan league.

## District Value
- Overnight berths and guarded corrals
- Dispute mediation over missing cargo
- Escort hiring boards for dangerous routes

## NPCs Commonly Present
- [[people/ylra-nine-knots|Ylra Nine-Knots]]
- [[people/torren-valechain|Torren Valechain]]

## Story Hooks
- Two rival caravans claim the same arch-right under conflicting old oaths.
- A missing courier's satchel appears at breakfast with tomorrow's date sewn into the strap.
