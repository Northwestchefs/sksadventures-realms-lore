# Sapmoon Greenhouse

## Why It Matters
A controlled cultivation hall that preserves rare medicinal cuttings and volatile moon-fed flora needed for high-order healing in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]].

## Control and Access
Entry is licensed by greenhouse stewards; unstable beds are sealed during eclipse phases and opened only with paired custodians.

## Campaign Use
Supports rescue missions during containment failures, ingredient procurement under deadline, and moral choices around experimental cures.
