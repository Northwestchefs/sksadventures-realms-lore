# Emberlane Spice Coils

## Description
A heated lane of braziers and ceramic coils where merchants roast fungus blends, oils, and medicinal spice packs.

## Signature Goods
- Cave saffron and emberpepper dust
- Pickled root ribbons and travel broth cakes
- Alchemical smoke spices for rituals and signaling

## NPCs Commonly Present
- [[people/faezra-smokeleaf|Faezra Smokeleaf]]

## Story Hooks
- A popular tea blend causes shared prophetic dreams among unrelated buyers.
- Someone replaces a spice shipment with harmless dust to force a protection contract.
