# Lanternhook Quay

## Description
A freight dock district of pulley towers and root-lifts where cargo rises from deep tunnels into Rootmarket's counting halls.

## What Happens Here
- Manifest checks and route stamping
- Locker rental for high-risk cargo
- Quiet after-hours crate transfers

## NPCs Commonly Present
- [[people/torren-valechain|Torren Valechain]]
- [[people/ylra-nine-knots|Ylra Nine-Knots]]

## Story Hooks
- A lift cage returns empty but drenched in seawater from nowhere near the coast.
- Dock lamps spell a warning pattern that only old smugglers recognize.
