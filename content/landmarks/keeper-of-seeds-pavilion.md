# Keeper of Seeds Pavilion

## Why It Matters
The legal and ritual seat for seed registries, lineage claims, and cultivation charters that determine what may be planted across [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]].

## Control and Access
Run by senior wardens with rotating witness circles to prevent any single faction from monopolizing seed law.

## Campaign Use
Supports legal hearings, political leverage over food and medicine, and heists aimed at altering lineage records.
