# Root-Coin Exchange

## Description
A vaulted hall of counting roots and crystal scales where contracts are notarized, coins are assayed, and trade trusts are enforced.

## Services
- Coin purity testing and mint marks
- Letters of credit and bonded escrow
- Debt consolidation and guarantor records

- A sealed escrow chest starts leaking moonlight and attracts hostile bidders.
