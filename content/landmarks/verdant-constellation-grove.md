# Verdant Constellation Grove

## Why It Matters
A ceremonial orchard in [[regions/callidae-districts/starlight-gardens|Starlight Gardens]] where trees are planted to mirror seasonal star patterns used for omen-reading and crop timing.

## Control and Access
Managed by the Keepers of Seeds under shared oversight from ritual gardeners and city elders in [[settlements/callidae|Callidae]].

## Campaign Use
Supports omen disputes, sabotage investigations, and navigation clues encoded in living grove geometry.
