# Waukeen

## Overview
Waukeen is commonly associated with trade, wealth, bargains, and mercantile ambition.

## Public Role
Temples and clergy often serve as brokers, lenders, arbitrators, and patrons of market stability.

## Campaign Use
Useful in stories involving contracts, debt, trade disputes, and ethical questions around profit.
